Recruitment & Job Description

1. Software Developer

Job Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and maintain software applications
  • Write clean, efficient, and reusable code
  • Test and debug software programs
  • Collaborate with team members and project managers
  • Maintain technical documentation

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Basic knowledge of software development concepts
  • Understanding of SDLC

Required Skills

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Basic knowledge of databases
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Good communication skills

2. HR Executive

Job Responsibilities

  • Handle recruitment and employee onboarding
  • Maintain employee records and HR documents
  • Assist in payroll and attendance management
  • Handle employee queries and grievances
  • Support HR policies and procedures

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources or Business Administration
  • Knowledge of HR practices and procedures
  • Understanding of labor laws

Required Skills

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Recruitment and coordination skills
  • MS Office proficiency
  • Organizational skills
